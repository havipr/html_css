About Us

The Reina Foundation works collaboratively to improve the education and well-being of children and their families.

Through funding and advocacy we develop solutions that seek to close the education and economical gap in Colombia.

The Foundation
Founder

Through his experiences, He developed a strong skillset in cross-cultural communications, adaptive thinking, and international commercial awareness.
